DDD Go by B.Balev

A meticulously crafted DDD modular monolith template, embodying principles of clean architecture, SOLID design, and separation of concerns. This structure promotes high cohesion within modules and loose coupling between them, facilitating ease of maintenance, scalability, and potential future microservices transition.
